Hi, I'm Melat!

CS undergrad at MIT

I study how language models store, share, and forget knowledge, and whether a model's account of its own reasoning can be made faithful and auditable. I work in Prof. Jacob Andreas's group at MIT CSAIL, currently on two threads: training models so their self-explanations stay consistent with their behavior, and recovering the latent scientific procedures models use to solve domain problems.

Selected work
